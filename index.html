About Me

Hello! My name is Alex, I’m 25 years old and passionate about technology, especially when it comes to creating immersive digital experiences. With over 5 years of experience as a game developer, I build robust solutions using Unreal Engine, focusing on multiplayer systems, dedicated servers, and the powerful Gameplay Ability System (GAS). Recently, I’ve expanded my expertise into web design, where I’ve been applying modern concepts using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I'm an enthusiast of clean code practices, version control with Git/GitHub, and my favorite development environment is JetBrains Rider — though I also have solid experience with Visual Studio and VS Code.
